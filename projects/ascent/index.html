Ascent


Ascent is set in the future where humans have colonized a distant alien planet. Humans have built a city despite the strange lifeforms there. They are still researching the resources that this world has to offer.

Ascent is a virtual space that was run through Mozilla Hubs. Giving players the ability to explore the world themselves. Mozilla Hubs is no longer supported.
